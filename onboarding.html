PARALINE

Welcome to Paraline

A transparent desktop audio visualizer that turns your screen edges into ambient motion. Your visualizer is already running — here is a quick guide to get started.

Tray Controls

Paraline lives in your system tray. Click the tray icon to open the quick controls menu.

  • Pause / Resume Freeze or restart the visualizer animation
  • Hide / Show Temporarily hide the overlay without quitting
  • Switch Themes Pick from eleven edge visualizer styles
  • Open Settings Fine-tune colors, sensitivity, and theme options
  • Quit Exit Paraline cleanly from the tray menu

Theme Preview

Each theme has its own visual identity and saved settings.

Keyboard & Quick Tips

  • Esc Close the tray context menu
  • Tray icon click Open pause, theme, and settings controls
  • Settings sidebar Use Hide, Pause, and Reload buttons for quick actions
